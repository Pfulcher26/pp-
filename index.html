The Man Behind the Mask

As my father always said, behind every mask there is a man. Therefore, allow me to take some time to explain a little about myself.

That's an image of me with The Cascadia Courier Collective, a local, barely-for-profit,bike delivery service in Portland, OR, aka my crew. As you can tell, I'm wearing a mask. Through the experiences I have accrued in my thus far, I have put on many masks, not in a disengenuous way. The masks have changed over time, and so has the person beneath in some small, immeasurable ways. To get to the point, my younger years mademe a more well-rounded, generous, and diverse person, and that's the programmer you'll get.

Why Programming?

In conclusion, I want to take a moment to think about the heart kid from Captain Planet. Yes, I don't even know the heart kid's name. Okay, I just googled it, praise be. It's Ma-Ti. So, Ma-Ti, as is commonly conferred upon, always seemed like the lamest planeteer. He couldn't really do anything. While everyone else was out moving lava, creating tornados, splitting the earth into, Ma-Ti was out making friends with monkeys. But from the current perspective, as one grows up, and enters into, becomes part of the world, and views it on its own terms, we see that Ma-Ti, as hackneyed as it sounds, was actually the most essential of all the planeteers, because he had possessed the power of the only element within the world that we as humans can control: the heart. As programmers, we are much the same. Though we manipulate extremely powerful tools now capable of altering, and in some cases superceding reality, our truest power lies in how we care for the world, and our place in it. We must strive to possess the power of Ma-Ti. We must strive to be refreshing human-beings.
