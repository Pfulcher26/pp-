The Man Behind the Mask

As my father always said, behind every mask there is a man. Therefore, allow me to take some time to explain a little about myself.

That's an image of me with The Cascadia Courier Collective, a local, barely-for-profit, bike delivery service in Portland, OR, aka my crew. As you can tell, I'm wearing a mask. In my life thus far, and the experiences I have accrued, I feel I have put on many masks, though not in a disengenuous way. The masks have changed over time, and so has the person beneath in some small, immeasurable ways. To get to the point, my younger years made me a more well-rounded, generous, and diverse person, and that's the programmer you'll get.
